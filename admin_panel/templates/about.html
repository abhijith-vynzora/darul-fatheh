Our Vision & Mission

Although Idukki is a district vast in area, it remains very backward in terms of population density and economic development. Since people must rely on daily wage labor for their livelihood, the state of both spiritual and material education is quite deplorable.

Darul Fateh was established in 1992 based on the realization that both forms of education are indispensable for the constructive survival of any community. Understanding the pulse of the times and designed to effectively communicate with the coming generations, the institution moves forward by offering admissions to students in various modern and scientific fields such as Dawa, Hifz, Boarding, Destitute/Orphan Care, and English Medium schooling.

It goes without saying that carrying these initiatives forward beautifully entails a heavy financial burden. This institution, which has no fixed income, operates solely with the help of generous hearts. As an institution that is more essential here than in any other region, the Almighty will not abandon anyone who aids in its growth. Wishing goodness to everyone.
