Frequently asked questions

  • What age group are the courses suitable for?
    Our programs are designed for various age groups, starting from children to adults. Age requirements vary depending on the course, such as Junior Hifz, Degree, or Ladies Section programs.
  • Are the classes conducted full-time or part-time?
    Darul Fatheh offers both full-time and flexible programs. Hifz and Thanwi courses follow a full-time structure, while community and ladies programs may have flexible schedules.
  • Are qualified scholars involved in teaching?
    Yes. All courses are guided by experienced and qualified Islamic scholars with strong expertise in Qur’an, Hadith, Fiqh, and classical Islamic studies.
  • Is hostel and boarding facility available?
    Residential facilities are available for selected full-time programs. Students stay in a safe, disciplined, and Islamic environment under proper supervision.
  • Can parents or guardians visit the students?
    Yes. Parents and guardians may meet students during designated visiting hours, following the academy’s guidelines to maintain discipline and student well-being.
